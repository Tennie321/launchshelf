# Module 2: ARC Reader Outreach Templates

## 12 Recruitment & Follow-Up Scripts

These templates help you recruit advance readers (ARCs) from free communities, even with zero followers.

---

### 1. Reddit ARC Recruitment Post (r/selfpublish)

**Title:** ARC readers wanted — [Book Title] — [Genre] — releasing [Month]

**Body:**

Hi everyone,

I'm looking for 15–20 advance readers for my upcoming book [Book Title], a [genre] novel releasing [date].

**The book:** [2–3 sentence hook]

**What you get:** A free eBook/PDF copy before release.

**What I ask:** An honest review on Amazon (no rating requirement, just your genuine thoughts) within the first week after launch.

**To apply:** Comment below or DM me with the last book you read and enjoyed.

Thanks!

---

### 2. Genre Discord DM

Hey! Saw you're active in [genre] discussions. I'm launching my book [Title] in [month] and putting together an ARC team. Would you be interested in a free advance copy in exchange for an honest review?

---

### 3. Facebook Group ARC Post

**Post text:**

ARC call for my upcoming [genre] novel [Title]! 🔥

Looking for 15 readers who want a FREE advance copy. Just leave an honest review on Amazon after launch.

Comment or PM me if interested!

---

### 4. ARC Welcome Email

**Subject:** Welcome to the ARC team for [Book Title]!

Hi [Name],

Thanks so much for signing up to read [Book Title]!

Here's your advance copy: [LINK]

**Your review matters most on launch day or the day after.** That's when Amazon's algorithm is watching most closely for new books. If you can post your review anytime in the first week, that's perfect.

**A few notes:**
- No pressure on rating — honest reviews only
- Tell your friends if you love it!
- DM me if you have any questions

Thank you for being part of this!

[Your Name]

---

### 5. ARC Check-in (2 weeks before launch)

**Subject:** Quick check-in — [Book Title] releases [Date]

Hi [Name],

Just checking in! [Book Title] releases on [Date]. If you've finished reading, reviews can go live starting [Date].

Your review makes a real difference for a new author. If you need more time, no worries at all.

Thanks again!

[Your Name]

### 6. Launch Day Reminder

**Subject:** It's live! [Book Title] is out now
