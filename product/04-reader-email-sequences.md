# Module 4: Reader Email Sequences

## Welcome Series (5 emails) + Launch Series (6 emails)

---

### Welcome Series (for new list subscribers)

**Email 1: Welcome + Lead Magnet**
**Subject:** Your free [lead magnet name] is here

Hi [Name],

Welcome! Here's your free copy of [lead magnet]: [LINK]

I'll send a few more emails over the next week with launch tips and book recommendations. If you'd rather not, no hard feelings — unsubscribe anytime.

Happy reading,
[Your Name]

**Email 2: Behind the Book**
**Subject:** Why I wrote [Book Title]

[Personal story about why this book exists — 3 paragraphs max]

**Email 3: Writing Process**
**Subject:** How I wrote this book in [X months]

[Tips and process, relatable content — don't sell]

**Email 4: Exclusive Excerpt**
**Subject:** Sneak peek — [Book Title] Chapter 1

[First chapter or best excerpt. Leave them wanting more.]

**Email 5: Pre-order / Launch Announcement**
**Subject:** [Book Title] is now available for pre-order

If you enjoyed the excerpt, the full book is live! [LINK]

[Name], thank you for being part of this journey.

— [Your Name]

---

### Launch Series (for the launch window)

**Email 1 (Launch Day):** IT'S LIVE
**Subject:** [Book Title] is out now!

Today's the day! Grab your copy here: [LINK]

Would love to hear what you think.

**Email 2 (Day 2):** Social Proof
**Subject:** What early readers are saying

[2–3 real quotes or excerpts from ARC reviews]

**Email 3 (Day 4):** The Story Behind
**Subject:** The chapter I almost cut

[Behind-the-scenes content. The thing you almost removed but kept.]

**Email 4 (Day 6):** Reviews Help
**Subject:** Quick favor?

If you've finished the book and enjoyed it, a quick Amazon review means the world. Even a few sentences: [LINK]

**Email 5 (Day 10):** What's Next
**Subject:** What I'm working on next

[Tease the next book. If you have one in the same world, include a cover reveal or blurb.]

**Email 6 (Day 14):** Final Follow
**Subject:** Last email about [Book Title]

[Wrap up the launch. Thank subscribers. Transition back to regular content schedule.]
