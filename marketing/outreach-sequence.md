# Cold Outreach Sequence — Partnership & Affiliate Outreach

## Who this targets (and why it's not spam)

For a $49 B2C product, cold-emailing individual authors is spam — low conversion, high risk to your domain reputation, and legally shaky. What works instead is **B2B partnership outreach** to people who already have your buyers' attention:

- Author newsletter owners (writing-craft & self-pub newsletters)
- Self-publishing podcast hosts & YouTubers
- Writing community moderators / Discord owners
- Author coaches & editors (Pro tier = commercial license, a real fit)

**Offer:** free Pro copy + 30–50% Gumroad affiliate commission. They earn, their audience gets a discount, you get distribution.

**Compliance (US CAN-SPAM):** these are business-relationship emails, still: use your real name and address in the footer, honor opt-outs immediately, never use deceptive subject lines. Send manually or ≤20/day from a warm Gmail — no blast tools on a new domain.

## Email 1 — Day 1 (first touch)

**Subject:** quick idea for [Newsletter/Podcast name]

Hi [First name],

I've been reading [Newsletter name] for a bit — the piece on [specific recent topic] was the kind of practical thing most self-pub content skips.

I make LaunchShelf, a 90-day book-launch kit for indie authors (launch plan, ARC outreach templates, KDP listing prompts). It's $49, and I'd rather split revenue with people authors already trust than run ads.

Two things, zero obligation:

1. A free copy of the full Pro kit for you — keep it either way.
2. If you think it's good, an affiliate link at 40% ($19.60/sale) plus a 20% discount code for your readers.

Want me to send the kit over?

[Your name]
[Your site] · [Physical mailing address] · Reply "no thanks" and I won't email again.
