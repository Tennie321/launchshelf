## Full Description

**You spent a year writing your book. Don't spend launch week guessing.**

Most indie books sell fewer than 100 copies — not because the writing is bad, but because "publishing" and "launching" are two different skills. LaunchShelf gives you the second skill in a box.

**What you get (instant download, Markdown + PDF):**

📅 **The 90-Day Launch Plan** — A day-by-day roadmap starting 12 weeks before launch. Every task in order with time estimates: building your ARC team, setting up your KDP listing, stacking reviews, and executing launch week hour by hour.

📨 **12 ARC Reader Outreach Templates** — Copy-paste emails and DMs to recruit advance readers from free communities (even with zero followers), follow up gracefully, and convert ARC copies into launch-day reviews.

🔍 **The KDP Listing Prompt Pack (25 prompts)** — Paste your manuscript details into any free AI tool (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini) and generate: a blurb that hooks, 7 backend keywords, category recommendations, editorial-style review pulls, and A+ content ideas.

💌 **Reader Email Sequences (11 emails)** — A 5-email welcome series for new subscribers and a 6-email launch series. Written to sound like an author, not a marketer.

📣 **30-Day Social Promo Calendar** — A month of post ideas with hooks and caption templates for Instagram, TikTok, and X, tuned for authors.

**Who it's for:** first-time and early-career self-published authors (fiction or nonfiction) launching on Amazon KDP, especially if you have no email list and no following yet.

**Who it's NOT for:** authors with established six-figure launch machines, or anyone looking for "get rich on KDP" hype. This is a work system, not a magic button.

**Guarantee:** 30 days, money back, no questions asked.
