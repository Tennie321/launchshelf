# Business Model — LaunchShelf

## The one-line model

Sell a $29–$99 digital launch kit to self-published authors on Gumroad, driven by free channels (Reddit, X, newsletter affiliates) — near-zero costs, ~87% margin after Gumroad fees.

## Why this niche (data, not vibes)

Gumroad's Writing & Publishing category has only ~226 products but ~$15,750 revenue per product and the **highest median sales of any category (41)** — high demand, thin supply. The main comparable launch package sells at $110+, leaving room at $49. Top Gumroad products share three traits: 4.4+ ratings, tight niche, priced above $20 — this product is designed to all three.

## Costs

| Item | Cost |
|---|---|
| Gumroad account & hosting of product | $0 (10% + ~3% processing per sale) |
| Landing page hosting (GitHub Pages / Surge / Vercel) | $0 |
| Domain (optional but recommended) | ~$12/yr |
| Formspree free tier (email capture, 50/mo) | $0 |
| Canva free tier (cover image) | $0 |
| **Total to launch** | **~$12** |

Per-sale take at $49: **~$42.60** after Gumroad's ~13%. Affiliate sales at 40%: **~$23** net to you.

## Pricing rationale

- **$29 Starter** — captures price-sensitive buyers; anchor for the middle tier.
- **$49 Complete (default)** — the real product. Above the $20 "quality floor" on Gumroad, well under the $110 competitor, impulse-buyable without a sales call.
- **$99 Pro** — commercial license for author coaches/editors. Costs nothing extra to deliver; 10–15% of buyers pick it and it doubles those orders. Classic good/better/best lifts average order value ~20–30%.

## Revenue projections (honest ones)

Assumptions: 5–10 hrs/week of marketing effort, no existing audience, no ad spend. Conversion benchmarks: cold landing-page → sale ≈ 1%; warm (Reddit value post / affiliate mention) ≈ 2–3%; Gumroad average order value ≈ $52 with tiers.

| Scenario | Month 1 | Month 3 | Month 6 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Low** (posts flop, no affiliates land) | $0–100 (0–2 sales) | $100–200/mo | $150–300/mo |
| **Base** (1–2 Reddit posts perform, 2 affiliates live) | $150–400 (3–8 sales) | $400–800/mo | $600–1,200/mo |
| **High** (a post goes semi-viral or a 10k-sub newsletter features it) | $500–1,000 | $1,000–2,000/mo | $1,500–3,000/mo |

**What the "$50–500/mo" label really means:** base case lands in it by month 2–3. Months of $0 at the start are normal, not failure. Anyone projecting $5k in month one for a cold-start digital product is lying to you.

### The math behind the base case (month 3)

- 2 Reddit value posts/mo → ~3,000 views → ~150 site visits → ~3 sales
- 3 X threads/mo → ~2,000 impressions → ~60 visits → ~1 sale
- 2 active affiliates → ~4 sales/mo (net $23 each)
- Email list (~120 subs by month 3, 6-email nurture) → ~2 sales/mo
- ≈ 10 sales ≈ **$470/mo gross, ~$390 net**

## Unit economics summary

| Metric | Value |

| Price (blended AOV) | ~$52 |

| Net per affiliate sale | ~$23 |
| Marginal cost per sale | $0 |
| Break-even | 1 sale (covers the domain) |

## Growth levers (in order of leverage)

1. **Affiliates** — every newsletter partner is a permanent, free salesforce. Target: 10 active by month 3.
2. **Reviews on Gumroad** — seed 5–10 free copies for honest reviews week 1; ratings drive Gumroad's internal discovery.
3. **The free checklist funnel** — email subs convert at ~2% over the nurture sequence and compound monthly.
4. **Product line extension (month 3+)** — "LaunchShelf for Nonfiction," genre-specific prompt packs, a $19 order-bump ("Blurb Lab"). Same audience, zero new marketing.

## Risks (read this section twice)

- **Distribution is the whole game.** The product is done; revenue is 100% a function of the 5–10 hrs/week of posting and outreach actually happening. Skip three weeks and revenue is $0.
- **AI-content saturation.** Buyers are rightly suspicious of low-effort AI products. The counter: real substance (the modules are genuinely detailed), real reviews, honest marketing.
- **Platform risk.** Gumroad fees or policies can change; the email list you own is the hedge — build it from day 1.
- **Refunds.** Expect 3–8% with a no-questions guarantee. Priced in above.
